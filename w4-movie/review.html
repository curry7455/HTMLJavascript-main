REVIEW

Your Name: February 12, 2024

Hereditary is a chilling horror movie that delves into the dark and twisted secrets of a family's ancestry. Directed by Ari Aster, the film takes viewers on a harrowing journey as the Graham family uncovers horrifying truths about their lineage, leading to devastating consequences.

The performances are outstanding, especially Toni Collette's portrayal of Annie Graham, whose descent into madness is both captivating and terrifying. The atmosphere is palpable, with tension building steadily throughout the film, culminating in a shocking and unforgettable climax.

I highly recommend Hereditary to fans of psychological horror, as it delivers an intense and thought-provoking experience that will linger long after the credits roll.

Trivia

  • The film's eerie score was composed by Colin Stetson, adding to the unsettling atmosphere.
  • Toni Collette's performance in the film received widespread critical acclaim, earning her several award nominations.
  • Director Ari Aster drew inspiration from his own experiences and family dynamics to create the story.
